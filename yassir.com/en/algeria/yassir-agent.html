Frequently asked questions

What is a Yassir Agent?

A Yassir Agent is a representative who helps users top up their Yassir Cash at various locations. As an agent, you can earn commissions on each transaction.

How can I become a Yassir Agent?

To become a Yassir Agent, simply fill out the application form on our landing page. After your application is reviewed and approved, you will receive further instructions to start.

What are the benefits of becoming a Yassir Agent?

As a Yassir Agent, you will earn extra income through commissions, have access to a large customer base, and receive support and training from Yassir.

Do I need a special license to become a Yassir Agent?

Yes, you will need a commercial license to operate as a Yassir Agent. Please provide your Commercial Licence Number during the application process.
