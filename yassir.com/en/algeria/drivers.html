Frequently asked questions

Should I present myself with my vehicle?

On the day you come to the Yassir headquarters, it is very important to present yourself with your vehicle so that our technician can proceed with a verification.

What time of the day can I work?

Choose a schedule and adapt it to your family and social life, but you can work 24/7, as the more you work, the more you increase your earnings as a driver.

How long will the training take?

The training will last about half a day, and the goal is to teach you how to use the Yassir driver application and answer any questions you may have about the service.

Where are the high-demand areas?

Where there are Yassir customers, there is demand! It's up to you to ensure good service. A satisfied customer is the best business strategy.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us through our 24/7 customer service at +212 707 713 670, and also via Facebook/Messenger. We'll be happy to respond to you!

What are the requirements to fulfill?

To become a driver, you must meet the following conditions: A driving license for over two years., Have a vehicle., Have a Smartphone.

What is the mode of payment?

Two modes of payment are available: Cash payment, the mode used up to now, and payment by credit card, with a TPE launched for some partners.

What is Yassir Cash ?

Yassir Cash is a digital payment solution developed by Yassir. It allows Yassir drivers to easily manage their financial transactions, such as commission payments, balance top-ups, and getting their balance refunded easily.
