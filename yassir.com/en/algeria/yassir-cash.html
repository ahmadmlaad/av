Frequently asked questions

Can I share credit with someone who does not have an account?

The person you are sharing credit with must have a Yassir Cash account. If they do not, the transfer will not go through, and no credit will be deducted from your balance.

How can I pay for rides or food deliveries with my Yassir Cash account?

Simply select your Yassir Cash account as the payment method when booking a ride or ordering food. The amount will be deducted from your balance automatically.

What happens if my account balance is not enough to cover the payment?

By becoming a Yassir partner, you will gain increased visibility, access to a larger potential customer base, and the opportunity to expand your geographical reach. Additionally, you will have access to our technological platform to effectively manage orders and deliveries.

Are there any discounts for using my Yassir Cash account for purchases?

Yes! Enjoy a 20% discount on all purchases made through your Yassir Cash account.

How do I top up my account using a bank card?

You can tap the top-up button on the app's home screen or go to the top-up options directly in Yassir Cash.

How long does it take for a top-up to appear in my account?

Bank card top-ups are usually instant, but some transactions may take a few minutes to process.

How do I top up my account via an agent?

To top up via an agent, find an available agent and log into your account. Select 'Top up,' then choose 'Agent Top-up.' Enter the amount, generate a QR code, and show it to the agent. After scanning the code, pay the displayed amount plus any fees. Once scanned, the funds will be added to your account instantly.

How long does it take for a top-up via agent to appear in my account?

Top-ups through an agent are instant.

What is a Yassir Cash PIN?

A Yassir Cash PIN is a secure 4-digit passcode known only to you that allows you to access your account and confirm transactions.

How is Yassir Cash kept secure?

We adhere to strict security standards to keep your account safe. To learn more, please visit our Privacy policy: https://yassir.com/en/privacy-policy.
