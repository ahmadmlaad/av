Frequently asked questions

How does Yassir Market Delivery work?

Yassir Market Delivery allows you to browse and order groceries online through our mobile app, select your items, and we'll bring your groceries right to your doorstep.

What areas do you serve for grocery delivery?

You can check if we deliver to your location by entering your address on our mobile app.

Is there a minimum order requirement for delivery?

Yes, there is a minimum order amount required for delivery. This amount may vary based on your location.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery fees may apply based on your location and order total. You can view the delivery fee during checkout before completing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery (where available).
