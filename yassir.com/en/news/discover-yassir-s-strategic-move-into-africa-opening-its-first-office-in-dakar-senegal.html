Discover Yassir's strategic move into Africa, opening its first office in Dakar, Senegal.

by Yassir

Share

Yassir announces its expansion into several African countries.

Yassir, the North African leader in on-demand services and high-impact multi-service technological solutions for populations, confirms its status as an international company by establishing itself in several other African countries.

Algiers, March 22, 2022 - After five years of existence, Yassir opens its first office in Dakar, Senegal, today. The company aims to export its successful model across the entire African continent.

Yassir also plans to continue its presence in Europe and North America, particularly in France, Germany, and Canada. In this regard, Yassir has established a technological hub in Berlin.

With a trusted relationship with over 5 million users and more than 50,000 partners in the Maghreb, Yassir intends to expand its wide range of solutions, including transportation services, last-mile delivery services, and other innovative payment and e-commerce solutions, to these new markets in West Africa.

Yassir's international expansion follows a recent fundraising of $30 million from a diverse group of investors.

"This international expansion was in our plan from the beginning of the Yassir adventure. We are, above all, a startup with an exponential growth model. We rely on these new markets to further develop and strengthen our position in the countries where we are already present," said Yassir's CEO and co-founder, Noureddine Tayebi.

"Today, we want to export this North African success model. We are convinced that the combination of technology and local talent can offer sustainable solutions to the populations of the countries where we are establishing ourselves," he concluded.

About Yassir:

Founded in 2017 by Noureddine Tayebi & Mahdi Yettou, Yassir develops several technological applications that have more than 5 million users today.

The company provides on-demand services such as transportation for people and delivery services, generating income for over 50,000 partners, including drivers, couriers, merchants, FMCG, and wholesalers.

Yassir's services are available in 30 cities worldwide, including Algeria, Morocco, and Tunisia, as well as Canada and France, with imminent expansion into West Africa.\n
